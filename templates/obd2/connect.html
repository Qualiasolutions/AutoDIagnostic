Connect to Vehicle

Connection Instructions

  1. Ensure your vehicle is turned off
  2. Locate the OBD2 port (usually under the dashboard on the driver's side)
  3. Connect the OBD2 adapter to the port
  4. Connect the USB end to your device
  5. Turn the vehicle ignition to the "ON" position (you don't need to start the engine)
  6. Enter your vehicle information below and click "Connect"
