How to Use the Automotive Diagnostic Assistant
Getting Started

This application helps diagnose car problems using your smartphone's camera and microphone. Follow these steps:

  1. Enter Vehicle Information - Provide your car's make, model, year, and mileage.
  2. Use Camera Mode - Point your camera at the problem area (warning lights, fluid leaks, visible damage).
  3. Use Voice Mode - Describe the symptoms you're experiencing (strange noises, performance issues, etc.).
  4. Review Results - The AI will analyze the data and provide a diagnosis with possible solutions.
Tips for Best Results
  • When using the camera, ensure good lighting and a clear view of the problem area.
  • For dashboard warning lights, take a clear photo of the illuminated light.
  • When describing symptoms, be specific about when they occur (e.g., "grinding noise when braking" rather than just "noise").
  • Include details about when the problem started and any changes in driving conditions.
Understanding Results

The diagnosis will include:

  • Possible issues with confidence levels
  • Severity ratings (low, medium, high, critical)
  • DIY repair instructions when appropriate
  • Professional repair recommendations
  • Important safety warnings
Important Disclaimer
Safety First

This Automotive Diagnostic Assistant is designed to provide general guidance and is not a substitute for professional mechanical advice or service.

Please be aware:
  • The diagnoses provided are based on limited information and may not be complete or accurate in all situations.
  • Always prioritize safety when dealing with vehicle issues.
  • If you're experiencing a serious issue (smoke, fire, loss of control, etc.), stop using the vehicle immediately and seek professional help.
  • Follow all safety precautions when attempting DIY repairs.
  • For critical systems like brakes, steering, airbags, or fuel systems, professional service is strongly recommended.

By using this application, you acknowledge that you are solely responsible for your safety and any actions taken based on the information provided.
